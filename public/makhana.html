About Us

Bringing You the Best Makhana Since 2008

MakSnack was founded with a simple mission: to bring the finest quality Makhana (Fox Nuts) to health-conscious snackers across India and the world. Our journey began in the fertile fields of Mithila, Bihar, where Makhana has been cultivated for centuries.

We work directly with local farmers, ensuring fair prices and sustainable farming practices. Every batch of our Makhana is carefully selected, roasted, and seasoned in our state-of-the-art facility to maintain the highest standards of quality and hygiene.
